RULES

RDM

(Random Death Match) You must have some sort of interaction and reason before killing someone

VDM

(Vehicle DeathMatch) You must interact with a person and have a valid reason to run them over or to ram into their vehicle continuously.

Green Zones

It's consider only inside the block around them ● Pillbox Hospital ● Vehicle Dealer(s) ● Mechanic Shop(s) ● All Apartment Interiors ● Courthouse

IN GAME CHAT RULES:

● All chat must be in English or Nepali. You must have a working microphone to roleplay on this server. Excessive use of /report chat is not permitted. /report chat is only to be used if talking with a member of staff quickly for OOC purpose, for example ‘ID 17 is spawning Items!’.

MG - METAGAMING

Metagaming: is the act of using/relying on OOC information by any third party software or ways that are not in-game.
